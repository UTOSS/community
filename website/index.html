Welcome to UTOSS.

Building the Open Future.

About Us

UTOSS is UofT's first student club dedicated to Open Source. Our goal is to foster the future open source leaders and let them thrive in the open source community.

Our Divisions

Software

Focused on making high impact sustained contributions to large software-based open source communities and projects.

Hardware

Building original hardware projects in open source, with a focus on RISC-V architectures.

Outreach

Responsible for all public facing aspects of the club, including our website and social media presence.

Join Us

To become a member, we ask that you:

  • Show up in person to our weekly meetings
  • Make meaningful contributions in your chosen division
